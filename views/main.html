Foreword

For several of the garden plots presented below, there are plenty of other possible intercrosses. However, I present the choices that seem the easiest to me when starting from scratch. Here's my decision process:

  • Minimize cross depth (i.e. Wild Seed x Wild Seed is better than Wild Seed x Cross Seed)
  • Minimize gathering hassle:
    1. Hunt seals seeds
    2. Blessed Harvest-able seeds (HW+)
    3. Hidden, not rare seeds ([Earth/Fire/etc]light Seeds)
    4. 100% Luck of the Pioneer-able seeds (only hidden item at their node)
    5. 50% Luck of the Pioneer-able seeds (two hidden items at the same node)
    6. One-per-hour, hard limit seeds
  • If alternate crosses are unavoidable, pick a valuable cross
